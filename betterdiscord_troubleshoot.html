    BetterDiscord Troubleshooting

    FAQ

    Answers to frequently asked questions.

    BetterDiscord not installing!

    BetterDiscord is in continuous development with bug fixes and improvement and at the moment it seems to be an uncommon glitch all we can say is keep trying to install! :(

    My BetterDiscord is GONE!

    Don't worry this is an easy fix it usually happens because of a Discord update.

    • All you have to do is go to the BetterDiscord site and download and re-install BetterDiscord again!

    • Now you're probably asking yourself "Will I loosee my Plugins/Themes?" NOPE everything stays just as is

    Can't Access Settings

    How to access Discords settings manually because of a theme/plugin problem.

    • Step 1: Do CTRL+, on your keyboard

    • Step 2: Disable/Remove the recently added Plugin/Theme/CSS

    • Step 3: Do CTRL+R on your keyboard

    BTTV Emotes Updates

    BTTV emotes are updated weekly by EhsanKia from the BetterDiscord server

    • You can check here if the emotes have been updated.

    FFZ Emotes Updates

    FFZ emotes are updated weekly by Pohky from the BetterDiscord server

    • You can check here if the emotes have been updated.

    Blacklisted Emotes

    We as a community hate emotes getting in our sentences without wanting to put them there so we have decided to blacklisted them.

    • Want to check if an emote has been blacklisted? Check here.

    • 3 letter emotes are blacklisted. LUL converts to LUUL

    • Common words such as STOP! are blacklisted

    Add my Server

    Want to add your server to the BetterDiscord server list?

    • Go to Discordservers.com

    • On the top right click "Add Your Server" then go through the steps to add your server

    • That's it! Now as soon as you see your server in the Discordservers.com list it should be on BetterDiscord also.

    Server list empty

    Your server list is empty in BetterDiscord? This could be one of two things

    • Discordservers.com is having issues on their end and we can't possible do anything to fix this.

    • Your internet connection is too slow or it's dead.
